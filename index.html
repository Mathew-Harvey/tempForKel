MarineStream™

Extending from our core value of innovation, is the commercialisation of state-of-the-art technology. MarineStream™ is purpose-built blockchain based, artificial intelligence enabled enterprise resource planning (ERP) system.

The cutting-edge technology platform enables Franmarine to take a life-cycle approach to underwater asset management, that is tailored to our customer needs. The platform provides standardisation, transparency, and auditable traceability across our broad range of sub-sea operations and industry sectors.

Whether it be highly regulated biofouling management, technical repair work, engineering hold points or remote Class survey, your organisation benefits from real-time remote accessibility to the underwater work site.

Franmarine, powered by MarineStream delivers unparallelled compliance assurance, operational efficiency, and asset management intelligence for the global maritime and sub-sea industries.

BIOFOULING MANAGEMENT USE CASE

From the selection of anti-fouling systems based upon the design and operational profile of your vessel, to all aspects of inspection, maintenance, control, compliance and quality assurance, transparency and traceability is key to effective biofouling management. Remotely Operated Vehicle

Franmarine own and operate a fleet of remotely operated vehicles (ROV's) as means providing an alternative to conventional diving capability across all industry sectors. We know through experience that successful ROV missions depend upon situational awareness and subject matter expertise relative to the task at hand.

Our ROV pilots draw upon the depth and breadth of Franmarine's expertise to ensure the success of the mission at hand, and that the objective of the mission is met.

All in service ROV's are integrated with MarineStream for standardised reporting, real time remote access and mission planning. Our terrestrial crawler fleet is specifically designed for hull, tank, and utility interventions.

These ROVs provide precise assessments of submerged and confined structures as well as mechanised in-water cleaning solutions. Magnetic wheels enable vertical surface inspections, reducing downtime and improving operational efficiency.

Certified by Class, equipped with MarineStream, innovative sensors, sonar, and enhanced 4K imaging to ensure clear, actionable data for informed decision-making, Franmarine's ROV capability is a service-in-itself.
